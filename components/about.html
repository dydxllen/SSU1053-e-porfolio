About SSU1053

SSU1053 Intro to Social Interaction explores the principles and processes of how people interact in various social contexts. The course covers communication, perception, group dynamics, culture, and the impact of technology on social behavior. Through lectures, discussions, and practical activities, students develop skills for effective interpersonal and professional relationships.

  • Understand key theories of social interaction
  • Analyze verbal and nonverbal communication
  • Apply concepts to real-world scenarios
  • Enhance teamwork and leadership abilities
